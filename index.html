Project on Yelp Data-set Challenge

We are finding the terms that are most commonly seen in reviews of a restaurant classified on the ratings. These terms are the important terms for starting a new venture in restaurant business of a particular city. This terms require attention to develop an existing or new business.Their weight-age describes their importance.

We are also focusing on the trends from the data of reviews. These trends can be useful in order to develop a new business for deciding the menu.

Results

The pie-charts represent the distribution percentage of terms (functionality of things in a restaurant) that help in getting lowest and highest stars in ratings.Further we are viewing the trends of food in Charlotte over the period of time. This altogether can be used to develop a new business or improve existing business.
